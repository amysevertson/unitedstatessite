Trivia

Fun Facts:

  • Arkansas is the only place in the United States where diamonds are actively discovered and mined
  • Arkansas is the nation’s leading producer of poultry and rice, producing half the nation’s rice
  • Little Rock, the capital city of Arkansas, is located directly in the center of the state
  • Sam Walton founded his Wal-Mart stores in Bentonville
  • Magnet Cove region contains 102 varieties of minerals

I am thinking tonight of the Southland, Of the home of my childhood days, Where I roamed through the woods and the meadows By the mill and the brook that plays; Where the roses are in bloom And the sweet magnolia too, Where the jasmine is white And the fields are violet blue, There a welcome awaits all her children Who have wandered afar from home.
