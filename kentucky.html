Trivia

Fun Facts:

  • The Kentucky Derby is the oldest continuously held horse race in the country
  • The first Kentucky Fried Chicken restaurant owned and operated by Colonel Sanders is located in Corbin
  • The song “Happy Birthday to You” was penned by two Louisville sisters
  • Mammoth Cave in Kentucky is the world’s longest cave system, with more than 400 miles explored
  • The very first American public performance of a Beethoven symphony was in Kentucky
