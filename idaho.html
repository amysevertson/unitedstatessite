Trivia

Fun Facts:

  • Idaho grows nearly one-third of America's potatoes
  • Idaho is known as the Lentil Capital of the World
  • Idaho's combined wilderness is over 4.7 million acres, which is greater than the US's three smallest states combined
  • At 7,993 feet deep, Hells Canyon in western Idaho is the deepest river gorge in North America
  • The total length of Idaho's rivers and waterways (over 107,000 miles) could stretch across the US thirty-eight times
