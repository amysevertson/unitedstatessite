Trivia

Fun Facts:

  • Noah Webster, the author of the first dictionary, lived in West Hartford
  • Connecticut is home to the first hamburger, Polaroid camera, color television, and helicopter
  • The USS Nautilus – the world’s first nuclear powered submarine was built in Groton in 1954
  • Connecticut never ratified the 18th ammendment
  • The Scoville Memorial Library in Connecticut is the oldest public library in the U.S.
  • Connecticut is one of the states that makes up the 13 colonies
