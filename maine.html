Trivia

Fun Facts:

  • Maine is home to 65 lighthouses and is also known as the lighthouse state
  • Maine is the single largest producer of blueberries in the United States
  • 90% of the country's lobster supply comes from Maine
  • Maine has over 2,000 islands, over 3,000 miles of coastline and 6,000 lakes and ponds
  • Maine is the only state with a single syllable name
