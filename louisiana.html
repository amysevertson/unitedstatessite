Trivia

Fun Facts:

  • New Orleans is known as the Jazz Capital of the world
  • Louisiana is the only state in the country with "parishes" instead of counties
  • Louisiana was part of the Louisiana Territory that the US bought from France in 1803 for $15 million
  • Louisiana was named after Louis XIV, the King of France
  • The patent for Tabasco Sauce was one of the first patents ever in the state of Louisiana. It was filed in 1870
