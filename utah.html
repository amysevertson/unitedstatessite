Trivia

Fun Facts:

  • The mountains near Salt Lake City, Utah average 500 inches of snowfall per year
  • The official state cooking vessel of Utah is the Dutch oven
  • Utah is the only state where every county contains some part of a national forest
  • Utah is a great vacationland with 11,000 miles of fishing streams and 147,000 acres of lakes and reservoirs
  • Utah is the Jello capital of the world; more jello is eaten in Utah than anywhere else in the world
