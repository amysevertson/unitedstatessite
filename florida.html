Trivia

Fun Facts:

  • Florida has the most golf courses of any state in America
  • Florida is the flattest state in America
  • There is 1,200 miles of sand beaches and 1,800 miles of coastline
  • Cape Canaveral is America’s launch pad for space flights
  • The only place in the world where crocodiles and alligators live together is the Florida Everglades
  • It also produces 70% of the oranges in the United States and supplies 40% of the world’s orange juice
