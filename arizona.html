Trivia

Fun Facts:

  • Arizona is one of the four states that make up the four corners, the only place in the U.S. where four states join at one shared corner
  • The official state neckwear is the bolo tie
  • The original London Bridge was shipped stone by stone and reconstructed in Lake Havasu City
  • The amount of copper on the state capitol building is equivalent to 4,800,000 pennies
  • The Saguaro cactus can only be found in Arizona's Sonoran Desert
  • The Grand Canyon is one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World and the only one in the United States

Where the golden sun is flaming Into warm, white, shining day, And the sons of men are blazing Their priceless right of way.

Come stand beside the rivers Within our valleys broad. Stand here with heads uncovered, In the presence of our God!

While all around, about us The brave, unconquered band, As guardians and landmarks The giant mountains stand.

Not alone for gold and silver Is Arizona great. But with graves of heroes sleeping, All the land is consecrate!

O, come and live beside us However far ye roam Come and help us build up temples And name those temples "home".
