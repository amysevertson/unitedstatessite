Trivia

Fun Facts:

  • A portion of Yellowstone National Park, the first national park in the country, is in Montana. The park has 1000-3000 earthquakes each year and is home to one of the Earth’s few super volcanos
  • There are more cattle in Montana than there are people
  • Montana’s rivers and streams provide water for three oceans and three of the North American continent’s major river basins
  • Every spring nearly 10,000 white pelicans with a wingspan of nine feet migrate from the Gulf of Mexico to Medicine Lake in northeastern Montana
  • Montana is home to the largest Grizzly Bear population in the lower 48 states
