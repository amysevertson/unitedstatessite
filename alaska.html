Trivia
Fun Facts:
  • Alaska has about 100,000 glaciers that cover 5% of the state
  • The Northern Lights can be seen in Fairbanks 243 days a year
  • There are more than 3,000 rivers and 3,000,000 lakes
  • Alaska is the largest state in the union (1/5 of the entire USA and twice the size of Texas)
  • Alaska has more than 100 volcanoes
  • Alaska has a larger coastline than the rest of the United States combined. It is roughly 34,000 miles long.
